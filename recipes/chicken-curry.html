Chicken Curry

Gloriously creamy and comforting indian cuisine staple that will surely become a "go-to" option for many many dinners to come!

With just a few fresh ingredients and some pantry staples even the most inexperienced cook can prepare this dish with ease and indulge in a hearty meal ready in less than 30 minutes.

What's more it can be customized to everybody's taste and food preferences, so vegetarians, and anybody looking for a meat-free option, don't you worry! Chicken in this recipe can be swapped for chickpeas, firm white cheese, or tofu without any difficulty.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken, diced on cubes 2cmx2cm in diameter
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1cm piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 200ml tomato puree
  • 400ml cream
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Add oil to the preheated pan and after it get's hot enough add to it chopped onion, garlic and ginger. Stir continuously until the onion turns translucent
  2. Next add tomato puree, curry powder and salt to the pan (optionally also a chili powder if you want the dish to be spicy) and stir until the sauce reduces a bit
  3. After the consistency of the sauce gets thicker, add diced chicken and stir until it's cooked through
  4. Add the cream to everything and wait until the sauce thickens
  5. Serve either with naan bread, rice or just by itself
