Tomato Pasta

One of the easiest pasta dishes for quick and simple dinned in style!

Made only with pantry staples and requiring low effort with an outstanding result.

It's so simple no elaborate description is needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp salt
  • 250g uncooked pasta

Steps

  1. Boil 750ml of water in a pot, add a teaspoon of salt to it
  2. Add pasta to the boiling water and follow instructions about cooking time that are on the packaging
  3. Preheat the pan and add the oil, wait until it gets hot
  4. Add chopped onion and garlic to the pan and stir continuously until the onion turns translucent
  5. Add tomato puree and salt to the pan, cook until the sauce reduces a bit and thickens
  6. When the pasta gets cooked, drain it and combine with the ready tomato sauce
